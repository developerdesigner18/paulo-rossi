01/07

the perpetrators

Totonero 1980 was a soccer match fixing scandal in Italian Serie A and B. The “masterminds” behind the scandal were two small businessmen, Alvaro Trinca and Massimo Cruciani.

02

the approach

02/07

the approach

Both perpetrators lived in Rome and were socially friendly with a lot of professional footballers. Trinca and Cruciani gained the trust of some of the footballers and the scheme was hatched.

06/07

a "mere" $2000

Paulo Rossi was one of the “other” players the two gamblers approached. Cruciani directly approached Rossi. Cruciani claimed, that he had given Rossi a “mere” two million Lire, roughly $2,000 in 1980 money.
